The following software packages are available for use online, download or purchase.
 
RiskAssess - risk assessments for schools
RiskAssess is a risk assessment system for high schools. It allows teachers and lab assistants to perform risk assessments and order experiments quickly and easily. It helps make schools safer and meet legal requirements.
 
Your Wedding Presents - a free, non-affiliated wedding registry
Your Wedding Presents is an elegant, online wedding registry. Unlike other registries, it is not owned by any store, and does not limit your gifts in any way.
 
WardWatch
Designed specifically for the Palm Organizers to aid medical staff involved in ward rounds. It records investigations, medications, dosages, and consultations. Developed as a joint effort with Torlesse Systems.

MedAccount
An accounting package for general practitioners (MDs).

Regarde
Graphs mathematical equations.

Safety Quiz
A multiple choice quiz engine and safety question database.

Fractal Magic
Plots Mandelbrot and Julia set fractal

Spectre
Internet chat application with a similar purpose to ICQ.
