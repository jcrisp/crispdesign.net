MedAccount Features

MedAccount:
  • Has a simple, easy-to-use screen layout





  • Automatically shows item numbers, charges and rebates





  • Prints receipts and invoices for consultations


  • Keeps track of unpaid accounts





  • Updates paid and unpaid totals


  • Backs up important files automatically


  • Is able to be customised to suit your needs


  • Is easy for sectretaries to learn


  • Is year 2000 compliant


Requirements:

Win95/Win98/NT/Win2000
486 or better computer
